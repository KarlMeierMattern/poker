#1 — Strongest

Royal Flush

A, K, Q, J, 10 — all the same suit. The unbeatable hand.

Extremely rare — roughly 1 in 649,740 hands. Splits the pot if two players both hold one.

Straight Flush

Five consecutive cards, all the same suit.

The highest card in the straight wins ties. A royal flush is simply an ace-high straight flush.

#3

Four of a Kind

Four cards of the same rank, plus any fifth card.

Also called "quads." Higher four-of-a-kind beats lower; kickers rarely matter.

#4

Full House

Three of a kind plus a pair.

Ranked by the three-of-a-kind first, then the pair. Jacks full of fours beats tens full of aces.
